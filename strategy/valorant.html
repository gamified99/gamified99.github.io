Valorant Strategy Guide

Master Your Aim
• Crosshair Always at Head Level
Don’t aim at feet or air—track heads like your life depends on it (because it does).
• Drill Daily, Even 15 Minutes
Deathmatch or Aim Lab—muscle memory needs reps, not vibes.
• Tap Before You Spray
Controlled bursts > panic full-auto unless you’re face-to-face.
⸻
Move With Purpose
• Stop Before Shooting
Moving while firing = missing. Learn counter-strafing and timing.
• Vary Your Peeks
Don’t be predictable—switch up timing, angles, and speed.
• Avoid Dry-Swinging Corners
Jiggle peek or clear with utility. Face-checking = free kill (for them).
⸻
Play Smart Utility
• Use It to Take Space
Smokes, flashes, mollies—don’t hoard them like skins.
• Chain It With Teammates
A good flash is wasted if no one swings. Time it together.
• Fake with Intent
Throw utility to sell a fake or bait rotations—don’t just spam.
⸻
Communicate Like a Pro
• Call What Matters
Enemy positions, spike info, utility used. Keep it short and sharp.
• Ping When Mic is Off
Silent? Cool. Still use your pings—info saves rounds.
• Stay Chill, Not Toxic
Tilt ruins comms. Keep energy up even if the scoreboard’s down.
⸻
Respect the Economy
• Buy With the Team
Don’t full-buy when everyone else is on a save. Sync up or fall behind.
• Eco With Intention
Sheriff, armor, and a dream can still clutch—if you don’t throw.
• Track Enemy Credits
Knowing if they’re broke or stacked can shape your next move.
